VIRTUAL EVENTS WITH AMBI

SPRING EQUINOX RITUAL AND GUIDED REIKI MEDITATION
Date: Mar 20, 2021

The Spring Equinox marks the first day of spring season and occurs when the sun passes the equator moving from the southern to the northern hemisphere. It has been celebrated for centuries to honor the balance between light and dark.

To honor this pivotal point of the year, Ambi invites you to celebrate with her in a Guided Reiki Meditation on the very day of the 2021 Spring Equinox.
