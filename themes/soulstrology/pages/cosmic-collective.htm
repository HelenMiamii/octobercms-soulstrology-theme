FOUNDER

AMBI KAVANAGH

Ambi Kavanagh (née Sitham) is the founder of Soulstrology.com.

Soulstrology was birthed through Ambi’s awakening over a decade ago which she describes as a *cosmic bitch slap*. She began to study astrology in depth and specifically the impact of celestial transits upon her chart, and thus her life. In doing so she realized there was a way of working with the planetary energies to overcome challenges, manifest her desires, and last but not least, to expand her consciousness and become a better member of the human race.

Ambi began to use Soulstrology to guide her coaching clients and mentees over the years with great success. She also created Soulstrology Soundbaths fusing cosmic consciousness with sound healing which have received high praise from goop, Vogue and The Hollywood Reporter. All of this inspired Ambi to democratize the teachings of Soulstrology through forming the Membership which to her is a beloved community. Her mission is to educate and empower the community to work with celestial energies to become best versions of themselves, live more fulfilling lives, and in turn contribute to the greater good.

Her life mantra has become Soulstrology’s motto: I’m With The Cosmos

ESSENTIAL OILS EXPERT

NADIA KAZOLIDES

Nadia is a holistic mamma and mystic living in Rome. She grew up in a spiritual community in London and studied in Los Angeles when both the London and LA spiritual-scape were VERY different.

Nadia facilitates transformational women’s circles often combining astrology, essential oils, and ancient rituals. Before becoming a doTerra Wellness Advocate, Nadia worked as a photojournalist and elementary school teacher in socio-economically developing countries.

Through connection with the oils, Nadia empowers women to find their wild and authentic voice by working with the oils. She has a deep love for the Earth and is committed to toxic free living. The Cosmic Collective has inspired Nadia to share the potency of the oils, especially combined with the powerful cosmos. Soulstrology has provided immense support for Nadia as she navigates motherhood and she is genuinely invested in sharing her experience of plant medicine with you.

SOUNDBATH CONDUCTER

KYLE LAM

Kyle Lam is a sound practitioner, whose roots stem from his musical background in performing chamber and classical compositions. Kyle discovered the world of sound healing in 2015 through his practice of kundalini yoga, connecting deeply with the sound of the gong and the impact it has on the physical body and the subconscious mind. Now, Kyle facilitates a variety of sound healing related events for local communities, guiding participants in a musical meditative journey to experience the medicinal benefits of sound therapy. His goal is to help bring community together, moreover, to raise the physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health of the community.

Kyle’s history with Soulstrology originated from receiving a Saturn return reading from Ambi herself, which catalyzed a series of transformation changes that ignited a new path towards his higher calling. Developing a deeper understanding of the cosmos, planets, and alignments, he uses his discoveries and knowledge from Soulstrology to enhance his sound bath experiences to the community. Come join Kyle as he gifts the Soulstrology community with his empowering sound bath experiences each month.
